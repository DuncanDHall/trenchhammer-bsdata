                     * Quicksilver Strike. The first time the Locus knocks an enemy Down or takes an enemy Out of Action in melee during its Activation, if the locus is not engaged in melee with any other enemy, it can immediately move up to its speed, and can end this movement in close combat. If it ends this movement in close combat, it can take an additional Fight Action, allowing it to attack with both of its Blades.
