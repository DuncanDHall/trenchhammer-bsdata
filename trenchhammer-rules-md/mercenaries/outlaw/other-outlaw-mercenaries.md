Battlekit
The Hired Gun can be equipped with any weapons, armour, or battlekit from the Necromunda Gang Shared Armoury or the Venators Gang Armoury each found in the Necromunda Gang Faction, except only up to 1 can use any Hunting Rig. You can purchase battlekit from these lists for the Hired Gun, but that battlekit cannot be used by any models other than Hired Guns unless you normally have access to the Armoury it is from or it is available to you from another Armoury for the same or lower price. You can still sell it normally.


When you recruit the Hired Gun, you must choose one of the following Specialties:
                     * Bruiser. The Hired Gun has +1 Melee and +0 Ranged.
                     * Shooter. The Hired Gun has +1 Ranged and +0 Melee.


When you recruit the Hired Gun, choose up to 2 of the following Specialties for it, or up to 4 if you paid 3 Glory.
                     * Agile. The Hired Gun’s movement speed increases by 1”, and it has +1 DICE to all Climb, Jump, and Diving Charge Success Rolls.
                     * Brute. The Hired Gun gains the STRONG Keyword.
                     * Duelist. Whenever an enemy Charges the Hired Gun, the Hired Gun can make a single melee attack with a non-HEAVY weapon against that enemy before it takes any further actions.
                     * Engineer. As an Action with a Success Roll, the Hired Gun can attempt to repair one ARTIFICIAL, MECHANICUS, or VEHICLE ally, or an ally with -2 or better Armour (excluding shields) within 1” of it. On a success, remove 1 BLOOD MARKER from that ally.
                     * Executioner. Once during each Turn, if the Hired Gun Downs or takes an enemy Out of Action in melee, it can move up to 3”, and can enter melee as if it Charged, then it can make a single extra melee attack.
                     * Explosives Expert. The Explosives Expert has NEGATE MINED. As an Action with a Success Roll with +1 DICE, the Hired Gun can mine a piece of terrain they alone are touching, no bigger than 8” x 8” (an 8” section of trench, a wall, a tree, a building etc). If successful, the terrain piece is now MINED.
                     * Grenadier. Increase the range of THROWN weapons used by the Hired Gun by 4”. It has +1 DICE to Hit with these weapons.
                     * Knife Fighter. The Hired Gun has +1 INJURY DICE with Close Combat Weapons and Throwing Knives.
                     * Massive (counts as 3 choices). The Hired Gun gains the LARGE and TOUGH Keywords.
                     * Master of Stealth. The Hired Gun gains the INFILTRATOR and STEALTH Keywords.
                     * Pistoleer. The Hired Gun has the following benefits while armed with Ranged Weapons with the PISTOL Keyword:
                     * If it is equipped with 2 Weapons with the PISTOL Keyword, it can take a Shoot ACTION with one and then immediately take a Shoot ACTION with the other.
                     * Add the ASSAULT and IGNORE OFF-HAND WEAPON Keywords to any weapons that have the PISTOL Keyword.
                     * Runner. The Hired Gun has +1 DICE to all Dash Success Rolls.
                     * Sniper. When the Hired Gun makes a ranged attack with a TWO-HANDED weapon, it has +1 INJURY DICE.
                     * Psyker (LIMIT: 1). The Hired Gun gains the PSYKER 1 Keyword. Select one Shared Psychic Discipline. The Hired Gun may purchase up to three powers from that Discipline (at normal cost). It can only use Psychic Powers while wielding a PSYCHIC weapon.
                     * Tactician. The Hired Gun gains the FIRETEAM Keyword, and can form a fireteam with any one other model in your Warband, chosen at the start of each battle.
                     * Terrifying. The Hired Gun gains the FEAR Keyword.
