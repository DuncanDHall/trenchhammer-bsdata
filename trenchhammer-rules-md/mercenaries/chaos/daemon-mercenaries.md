Battlekit
The Daemon Prince is always equipped with Daemonic Hellplate, a Hellforged Weapon, and an Infernal Cannon, included in its price and statistics above. You cannot change its battlekit in any way.
                     * Hellforged Weapon. Melee, +1 INJURY DICE, HEAVY, TWO-HANDED. When attacking, choose to either make an extra attack against a different enemy or gain AMOUR PIERCING 1.
                     * Infernal Cannon. 24”, +1 INJURY DICE, BLAST 2”, HEAVY, SHRAPNEL, TWO-HANDED


When you recruit a Daemon Prince, you must choose one of the following Types. If your Warband leader has the KHORNE, NURGLE, SLAANESH, or TZEENTCH Keyword, you must choose the corresponding option or Daemon Prince of Chaos.
                     * Daemon Prince of Darkness. The Daemon Prince and all allied models within 6” of it have the STEALTH Keyword.
                     * Daemon Prince of Khorne. The Daemon Prince’s melee attacks have an additional +1 INJURY MODIFIER, and it gains the KHORNE Keyword.
                     * Daemon Prince of Nurgle. The Daemon Prince treats Down results as Minor Hits (does not affect Down results that replace Out of Action via TOUGH Keyword), and it gains the NURGLE Keyword.
                     * Daemon Prince of Slaanesh. The Daemon Prince gains +1” movement speed, +1 DICE to all Dash Success Rolls, and the SLAANESH Keyword.
                     * Daemon Prince of Tzeentch. The Daemon Prince’s ranged attacks have an additional +1 INJURY MODIFIER, and it gains the TZEENTCH Keyword.


Abilities
                     * Daemonic Resistance. Injuries rolled against the Daemon Prince have -1 DICE.


Upgrades
                     * Wings. The Daemon Prince can be given wings for an additional +1 Glory, granting it 8” movement and FLYING.


Keywords
DAEMON, ELITE, LARGE, STRONG, TOUGH
