A Grey Seer is equipped with Power Armour and a Warpstone Staff, included in its cost and statistics above. You cannot change its battlekit in any way.

Battlekit
The Skaven Rat has no battlekit, but it is always considered to be equipped with a Close Combat Weapon. You cannot change its battlekit in any way.


Abilities
                     * Dodge. All attacks against the Skaven Rat have -1 DICE to Hit.
                     * Expendable. The Skaven Rat is not counted as part of your Warband for the purposes of Morale.
                     * Pet. Select one other non-ARTIFICIAL, non-BEAST model in your Warband at the start of the battle. The Skaven Rat forms a FIRETEAM with that model. That model is its owner.
                     * Skitter. The Skaven Rat has +1 DICE to all Climb, Dash, Jump, and Diving Charge Success Rolls.
                     * Strength in Numbers. The Skaven Rat has +1 DICE to Hit with melee attacks made against any enemy that has at least 2 of the Skaven Rat’s SKAVEN allies within 1” of it.

BEAST, NO PROMOTION, SKAVEN, STEALTH
