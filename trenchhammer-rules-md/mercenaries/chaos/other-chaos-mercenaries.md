Battlekit
Each Blessed Blade is equipped with Standard Armour and a Commune Blade. You cannot change its battlekit in any way.
                     * Commune Blade. Melee, +1 INJURY DICE, VICIOUS 11, CRITICAL, TWO-HANDED


Abilities
                     * Attuned In Purpose. Blessed Blades come in pairs. They are always in a FIRETEAM with each other. While they are both in close combat with the same enemy, they both have +1 DICE to Hit with melee attacks against that enemy. If one of them is a casualty or is otherwise removed from your Warband, the other cannot be fielded until you recruit a replacement, which costs 2 Glory. They share Promotion Dice, and are promoted to Elite as if one model, taking only one Elite slot in your Warband. They earn XP together and learn skills together. They suffer Trauma and Battle Scars individually.
                     * Cut Them Down. When an enemy in close combat with the Blessed Blade Retreats, the Blessed Blade has +1 DICE to Hit and Injure for its free attack against that model.
