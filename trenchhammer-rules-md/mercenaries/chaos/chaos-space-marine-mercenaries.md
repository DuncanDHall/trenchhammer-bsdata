Battlekit
The Master of Possession is equipped with Power Armour, included in its statistics above, as well as a Bolt Pistol and a Staff of Possession. You cannot change its battlekit in any way.
                     * Bolt Pistol. 12”, VICIOUS 11, CRITICAL
                     * Staff of Possession. Melee, +1 INJURY DICE against targets with DAEMON or PSYKER Keyword, ARMOUR PIERCING 1, PSYCHIC.


The Master of Possession knows the following Psychic Powers:
                     * Possession (Effect). No Range, Self, Next Activation, the caster has +1 INJURY DICE with melee attacks, and if it takes an enemy ELITE model Out of Action with a melee attack, you can place a Chaos Spawn Mercenary on the table under your control where that enemy was located. The Chaos Spawn does not count towards Morale or join your Warband, and the original model is treated normally for the post-game sequence. You can have only up to one Chaos Spawn on the table from this or any similar effect at a time.
                     * Sacrifice (Effect). 12”, One Ally and Self or One Other Ally, Immediate, the first target suffers 1 BLOOD MARKER, then remove D3 BLOOD MARKERS from the second target.
                     * Siphon Life (Attack). 18”, IGNORE COVER, IGNORE ARMOUR, -1 INJURY DICE, PSYCHIC, RISKY, remove 1 BLOOD MARKER from the caster on a hit that causes any BLOOD MARKERS or that takes the target Out of Action.


Abilities
                     * Black Carapace. The Master of Possession treats any Down result from the Injury Roll Table as a Minor Hits instead. This does not apply to Down results that already replaced another result, such as with TOUGH.
                     * Daemonkin. Friendly DAEMON models within 6” of this model have +1 DICE to all Dash Actions.
