# Campaign Content

Mission Tables
Use these Mission Tables instead of those found in the Trench Crusade rulebook. Bolded missions are from the Trench Crusade rulebook.
Games 1-3
Roll D6 to determine which Scenario to play:
1. Claim No Man’s Land
2. The High Ground
3. Supply Raid
4. Meatgrinder
5. Toxic Crossing
6. The player with the fewest Campaign VP chooses one of the Scenarios listed above. If tied, roll-off and the winner chooses.
Games 4-8
Roll D6 to determine which Scenario to play:
1. Hunt for Heroes
2. Escort Supplies
3. Industrial Zone
4. High Speed (or Claim No Man’s Land if appropriate terrain is not available)
5. Spec Ops
6. The player with the fewest Campaign VP chooses one of the Scenarios listed above. If tied, roll-off and the winner chooses.
Games 9-11
Roll D6 to determine which Scenario to play:
1. From Below (call Hell Ticks “Xenos Beasts” instead)
2. Industrial Zone
3. Spec Ops
4. Fragile Bounty
5. The Ritual
6. The player with the fewest Campaign VP chooses one of the Scenarios listed above. If tied, roll-off and the winner chooses.
Game 12
Roll D3 to determine which Scenario to play:
1. The Great War
2. The Inferno
3. The player with the fewest Campaign VP chooses one of the Scenarios listed above. If tied, roll-off and the winner chooses.

Exploration Skills
Use these descriptions for Exploration Skills instead of those found in the Trench Crusade rulebook.
* Duplicate. After you roll, select any Exploration Die and add another die with an identical result, including any modifications, to your total.
* Careful. You may roll 1 fewer Exploration Die, to a minimum of 1.
* Circle Back. Modify one Exploration Die result by -1.
* Extra Dice. You may roll 1 extra Exploration Die.
* Lucky. Roll an extra Exploration Die that is paired with one of your other dice. After you roll, choose one die in the pair to keep and one die in the pair to discard.
* Reroll. Reroll any single Exploration Die once.
* Seek. Modify one Exploration Die result by +1.
* Set Dice. Before rolling, select any one Exploration Die and set it to a number you want instead of rolling it.
