# Shared Psychic Powers

Use the numbers to randomize powers when needed, or if you want to.
1. Biomancy Discipline
                     1. Bio Lightning (5 credits, Attack). 12”, +1 INJURY DICE, PSYCHIC, RISKY
                     2. Endurance (5 credits, Effect). 6”, Self or One Ally, Immediate, remove 1 BLOOD MARKER from the chosen model.
                     3. Haemorrhage (3 Glory, Effect). -1 DICE, 12”, One Enemy, Immediate, the target suffers 3 BLOOD MARKERS. On a failure, the caster suffers 1 BLOOD MARKER.
                     4. Iron Arm (5 credits, Effect). No Range, Self, Next Activation, injury rolls against the caster have -1 INJURY MODIFIER, stacking with armour up to -3.
                     5. Life Leech (10 credits, Attack). 12”, IGNORE ARMOUR, PSYCHIC, RISKY. If this attack causes any BLOOD MARKERS or takes the target Out of Action, remove one BLOOD MARKER from the attacker.
                     6. Warp Speed (5 credits, Effect). 12”, Self or One Ally, Target Activation, the target has +2” movement speed.
 2. Divination Discipline
                     1. Foreboding (5 credits, Effect). -1 DICE, 6”, Self or One Ally, End of Turn, whenever an enemy attempts to Charge the target, the target can make either a ranged attack before that Charge, or a melee attack afterwards, against the Charging model in either case.
                     2. Misfortune (5 credits, Effect). 12”, One Enemy, Immediate, the target gains D3 STUN MARKERS.
                     3. Perfect Timing (3 credits, Effect). 12”, Self or One Ally, Target Activation, the target has IGNORE COVER and IGNORE LONG RANGE when making ranged attacks.
                     4. Precognition (5 credits, Effect). 6”, Self or One Ally, End of Turn, all attacks against that model have -1 DICE to Hit.
                     5. Prescience (7 credits, Effect). 6”, Self or One Ally, Immediate, the target gains a BLESSING MARKER.
                     6. Scrier's Gaze (4 Glory, Effect). No Range, Self, Special, for the next D6+1 Activations this Turn, unless another model has already successfully used this power this Turn, you choose which model will activate next after each Activation, following the normal rules (turn order, etc.). If you choose a model in a Fireteam, the model's owner decides if it will activate as a Fireteam or individually. On a failure, this model instead must roll on the Injury chart, ignoring armour. This power cannot be used while within 1” of an enemy.
3. Pyromancy Discipline
                     1. Fiery Form (15 credits, Effect). No Range, Self, End of Turn, Injury rolls against the caster are made at -1 DICE and its melee weapons gain the FIRE Keyword.
                     2. Fire Shield (7 credits, Effect). 6”, Self or One Ally, Immediate and End of Turn, each enemy in close combat with the target immediately suffers 1 BLOOD MARKER, and each time an enemy enters close combat with the target and after each time an enemy attacks it in melee, that enemy suffers 1 BLOOD MARKER. An enemy with NEGATE FIRE is immune to this power.
                     3. Flame Breath (20 credits, Attack). 12”, AUTOMATIC 2, -1 INJURY DICE, IGNORE COVER, IGNORE LONG RANGE, IGNORE ARMOUR, FIRE, PSYCHIC, RISKY
                     4. Molten Beam (3 Glory, Attack). 12”, IGNORE ARMOUR, FIRE, PSYCHIC, RISKY. When shot, draw a straight 12” line that is 1mm wide from the attacker. On a hit, all models along that line are hit. On a miss, the attacker is hit instead.
                     5. Spontaneous Combustion (15 credits, Effect). 12”, One Enemy, Immediate, the target suffers 2 BLOOD MARKERS. A target with NEGATE FIRE is immune to this power.
                     6. Sunburst (10 credits, Effect). -1 DICE, 3”, Each Model Besides Self, Immediate, each target must roll on the Injury table. This injury is considered to have the FIRE and IGNORE ARMOUR Keywords. This power cannot be used while within 1” of an enemy.
4. Technomancy Discipline
                     1. Blessing of the Machine God (5 credits, Effect). 6”, Self or One Ally, Target Activation, the target has +1 INJURY DICE with one weapon of your choice.
                     2. Fury of Medusa (2 Glory, Attack). 24”, BLAST 5”, PSYCHIC, RISKY. On a miss, the caster falls Down.
                     3. Machine Flense (5 credits, Attack). 12”, IGNORE ARMOUR, -1 INJURY DICE against non-ARTIFICIAL, non-VEHICLE models, PSYCHIC, RISKY, STUN
                     4. Objuration Mechanicum (15 credits, Effect). 8”, One Enemy, Immediate, choose one ranged weapon carried by the target. The target must make an attack with it against itself, but it does not add its Ranged skill to that attack if it is positive. This power cannot be used while in close combat.
                     5. Psysteel Armour (10 credits, Effect). 12”, Self or One Ally, End of Turn, choose one Armour battlekit (but not a shield) carried by the target. That Armour’s penalty to Injury rolls improves by -1, stacking to a maximum of -3.
                     6. Reforge (7 credits, Effect). 6”, Self or One Ally, immediate, if the target has the ARTIFICIAL, MECHANICUS, or VEHICLE Keyword, or is a model with at least -2 worth of Armour (besides shields), remove D3 BLOOD MARKERS from it.
5. Telekinesis Discipline
                     1. Assail (10 credits, Attack). 12”, ASSAULT, +1 INJURY MODIFIER, PSYCHIC, SHRAPNEL, RISKY
                     2. Crush (15 credits, Attack). 24”, IGNORE ARMOUR, PSYCHIC, RISKY
                     3. Gate of Infinity (15 credits, Effect). -1 DICE, 24”, Point, Immediate, choose a location that is not on or entirely surrounded by Impassable Terrain. Remove the caster from the battlefield and place it back on the battlefield at that point.
                     4. Shockwave (10 credits, Effect). 6”, Each Model Besides Self, Immediate, each target must roll on the Injury table, with +1 INJURY DICE. This injury has IGNORE ARMOUR, NONLETHAL, and STUN MARKERS. The targets are then each pushed D3” directly away from the caster unless they are LARGE.
                     5. Telekine Dome (7 credits, Effect). No Range, Self, End of Turn, Injury rolls against the caster and each ally within 3” of it have -1 DICE.
                     6. Vortex of Doom (2 Glory, Attack). 6”, IGNORE COVER, IGNORE ARMOUR, RELOAD, BLAST 5”, PSYCHIC, RISKY, SCATTER
6. Telepathy Discipline
                     1. Hallucination (2 Glory, Effect). 8”, One Enemy, Immediate, choose one weapon carried by the target. The target must make an attack with the chosen weapon against the target of your choice (it must be a legal target for the attack). On a failure, the target can instead make an attack with any one of its weapons against any target of its choice. This power cannot be used while in close combat.
                     2. Invisibility (10 credits, Effect). No Range, Self, Special, if any piece of scenery the caster is touching can block line of sight in any direction, even partially, regardless of whether an enemy currently has a clear line of sight, then enemies cannot target the caster with ranged attacks or Charges. Weapons with the Keyword BLAST affect it as normal if it is in the radius of the weapon. This effect ends if this caster moves from its exact position in any way, it makes a Ranged Attack, or an enemy model comes within 1.5” of it.
                     3. Mental Fortitude (3 credits, Effect). No Range, No Targets, End of Turn, you have +1 DICE to your Morale Test at the end of this Turn, if any.
                     4. Psychic Shriek (5 credits, Attack). Melee, IGNORE ARMOUR, PSYCHIC, RISKY, STUN
                     5. Puppet Master (10 credits, Effect). -1 DICE, 12”, One Enemy, Immediate, the target must move D6” in the direction of your choice. This movement allows free attacks as if it were a Retreat Action. This power cannot be used while in close combat.
                     6. Terrify (5 credits, Effect). Melee, One Enemy, Immediate, the target must move D6” directly away from the caster. This movement can bring the target into or out of melee, and counts as a Charge and/or Retreat as appropriate. A model that ignores the FEAR Keyword is immune.
