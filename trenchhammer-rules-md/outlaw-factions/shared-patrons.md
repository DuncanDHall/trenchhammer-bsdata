# Shared Patrons

Shared Imperial Patrons
Patron: Administratum Prefectus
Or a similar high ranking member of the Adeptus Administratum.
                     * Arrest Order. At the start of the battle, choose one enemy model. This model has +1 DICE to Hit and +1 INJURY DICE that model during this battle with all attacks, and it can choose to gain +1 DICE to any Dash Success Roll, but if it does and succeeds, it must move in as close as possible to the chosen enemy as its movement allows.
                     * Legal Action. At the start of the battle, after the deployment, select one enemy model. You can move this model up to 6” in any direction you wish, though not into Melee combat, into impassable terrain, or out of the battlefield.
                     * Punishable Offense. Whenever an enemy successfully Charges this model, that enemy gains a BLOOD MARKER.
                     * Red Tape. Any enemy Charging this model can only use their base Movement characteristics and not add a D6 to their charge distance.
                     * Restraint Protocols. Enemies cannot Retreat from this model.
                     * Threat Response. Once during each Turn, when an enemy ends its movement within 12” of this model, this model may immediately make an attack with a ranged weapon against that enemy if otherwise able.
Patron: Ecclesiastic Cardinal
Or a similar high-ranking member of the Ecclesiarchy.
                     * Ardent Eradication. When this model causes a Down enemy to roll on the Injury chart with a ranged attack, you may set 1 Die to 6 and roll the rest as normal.
                     * Ecclesiastic Inspiration (Adepta Sororitas Warband Only). At the start of each battle that this model participates in, you gain 1D3 Miracles.
                     * Fervent Brawl. This model is always considered to be equipped with a Close Combat Weapon if it has a free hand in melee, and its attacks with this weapon have +1 DICE and +1 INJURY DICE.
                     * Rally the Flock (Adeptus Ministorum Warband Only). At the end of each battle that this model took part in, you gain D3+1 Flock points.
                     * Redeemed Through Fire. This model’s attacks with the FIRE Keyword have +1 INJURY DICE.
                     * Unwavering Devotion. Whenever this model rolls on the Elite Trauma Chart, you can roll twice and choose one of the results.
                     * Zealous Persecution. Whenever this model takes an enemy Out of Action, this model gains 1 BLESSING MARKER.
Patron: The Indomitus Crusade
Roboute Guilliman or one of the other leaders of the Crusade.
                     * Adaptive Tactics. Select any skill you want from the Wild Card Skills.
                     * Medical Talent. Once after each battle, when this model or another in your Warband rolls on the Elite Trauma Chart, or makes a casualty test, you can reroll. The second result stands.
                     * Organizational Talent. While this model is in your Warband, increase the LIMIT of one piece of battlekit you are allowed to take by 1. Each piece of battlekit can be chosen only once.
                     * Supply Shipment: Armour and Equipment (LIMIT: 1). The cost of any suit of armour or equipment worth 15 credits or more is reduced by 5 credits if the model with this skill is part of the Warband. Note that weapons bought with this lower cost will make it easier for you to fit under the Threshold value.
                     * Supply Shipment: Melee Weapons (LIMIT: 1). The cost of any melee weapon worth 10 credits or more is reduced by 5 credits if the model with this skill is part of the Warband. Note that weapons bought with this lower cost will make it easier for you to fit under the Threshold value.
                     * Supply Shipment: Ranged Weapons (LIMIT: 1). The cost of any ranged weapon worth 20 credits or more is reduced by 5 credits if the model with this skill is part of the Warband. Note that weapons bought with this lower cost make it easier for you to fit under the Threshold value.
Patron: Lord Inquisitor
When you select this patron, choose either Puritan or Radical.
                     * Bound Entity (Radical Only). The model gains the DEEP STRIKE Keyword.
                     * Highly Placed Ally. This model gains the Set Dice Exploration Skill.
                     * Inquisitorial Honor (non-Inquisition Warband Only). At the end of each battle, you gain 1 Glory if this model participated.
                     * Master of Spies (Inquisition Warband Only). While this model is in your Warband, you can roll twice for your Shadow Operation at the start of each battle, choosing one result.
                     * Secret Forces. While this model is in your Warband, increase the LIMIT of one type of Troop you are allowed to take by 1. You cannot choose a model with a LIMIT of 1 or that has the LARGE Keyword, and each Troop type can be chosen only once.
                     * Trained by a Legend. This model gains the INFILTRATOR and STEALTH Keywords.
                     * Upholder of the Creed (Puritan Only). You have +1 DICE to Morale tests while this model is on the battlefield and not Down.
                     * Void Trader. This model gains the Lucky Exploration Skill.
Shared Chaos Patrons
Patron: Chaos Undivided
The concept of Chaos at large, or a Daemon Prince such as Be’lakor.
                     * Daemonic Whispers. With a successful Success Roll this model can force any single enemy model with 24" that is touching any Cover, but not in Melee combat, to move 1", leaving the Cover. The chosen model is moved by your opponent, and cannot take the model into Melee combat or into other Cover.
                     * Diabolist (Psyker Only). This model’s psychic powers that cause Injuries (including attacks) have +1 INJURY DICE.
                     * Exalted Possession (non-Daemon Only). This model gains the DAEMON Keyword, its movement speed is increased by 2”, and all injury rolls made against it have -1 DICE, but it gains 1 BLOOD MARKER at the end of each of its Activations.
                     * Exalted Possessor (Daemon Only). With a successful Success Roll this model can force any opponent it is in melee combat with to make one of its own Melee attacks against itself. You can choose which weapon to use.
                     * Hate-Fuelled Demagogue. This model and each friendly model within 6” of it gain the CRITICAL Keyword on all attacks they make.
                     * Master Of The Union. As an Action with a Success Roll, this model can choose one other friendly DAEMON within 6” that it can see. If this model is a DAEMON, it must choose a model that does not share any of the following Keywords with it: KHORNE, NURGLE, SLAANESH, TZEENTCH, UNDIVIDED. If it succeeds, remove 1 BLOOD MARKER from the chosen model. If a BLOOD MARKER is removed in this way, the chosen model gains a BLESSING MARKER.
                     * Profane Zeal. During an Activation in which it Charged, this model has +1 DICE to Hit and +1 INJURY DICE with melee attacks.
Patron: Chaos Warlord
Abbadon The Despoiler or another powerful Heretic Astartes warlord.
                     * Flames of Spite. With a successful Risky Success Roll, this model can choose one enemy within 6” that it can see. That model must roll on the Injury chart. This injury has the FIRE Keyword.
                     * Lord of Terror. This model gains the FEAR Keyword.
                     * Merciless Overseer. Friendly non-Elite, non-mercenary models within 3” of this model have +1 DICE to Hit with all attacks.
                     * Paragon Of Hatred. This model’s melee attacks hit each enemy it is in melee with.
                     * Trusted War Leader (Leader Only). At the end of each battle, you earn an additional 1 Glory.
                     * Veteran Raider. Enemy models never get free attacks against this model when it Retreats, and this model can move out of Melee Combat using its Standard Move, Charge, or Dash.
Patron: Khorne
Or a powerful Khorne Daemon.
                     * Aspect Of Death. This model’s attacks have +1 DICE to Hit and to Injure against Down enemies.
                     * Brazen Hide. This model’s armour improves by -1, stacking up to -3.
                     * Devastating Blow. Whenever this model hits with a melee attack, the target also suffers a STUN MARKER.
                     * Glory Of Battle. This model’s movement speed increases by 2”.
                     * Immense Power. Injuries caused by this model’s melee attacks have a +1 flat bonus and cannot have -DICE applied to them.
                     * Rage Incarnate. When this model is taken Out of Action, before removing it from the table, it can make a single melee attack against an enemy it is in close combat with. BLOOD MARKERS cannot be spent on this attack's Success Roll.
Patron: Nurgle
Or a powerful Nurgle Daemon.
                     * Acidic Ichor. This model’s melee attacks gain ARMOUR PIERCING 1, stacking with other sources of ARMOUR PIERCING.
                     * Arch-Contaminator. As long as at least one INFECTION MARKER is spent, this model needs to spend 1 fewer BLOOD MARKERS/INFECTION MARKERS to cause a Bloodbath.
                     * Foul Effluents. Whenever an enemy model activates while within 1” of this model, that enemy suffers a BLOOD MARKER. A model with NEGATE GAS is not affected.
                     * Heaving Mass. This model gains the LARGE Keyword if it did not already have it, and whenever it successfully Charges one or more enemies, one of those enemies gains a STUN MARKER.
                     * Overflowing Fecundity. At the start of each of this model’s Activations, remove 1 BLOOD MARKER from it.
                     * Plague Fly Hive. This model and each friendly NURGLE or CHAOS CULT model within 3” of it have the STEALTH Keyword.
Patron: Slaanesh
Or a powerful Slaanesh Daemon.
                     * Faultless Duellist. This model scores a Critical Hit with melee attacks on a result of 10 or higher.
                     * Glutton For Punishment. BLOOD MARKERS cannot be spent on this model’s attacks. In addition, while this model has 3 or more BLOOD MARKERS, it has +1 DICE to Hit with all attacks.
                     * Intoxicating Musk. Enemies within 1” of this model have -1 DICE to Hit with melee attacks. Models wit hNEGATE GAS are immune.
                     * Loathsome Grace. This model’s movement speed increases by 2”.
                     * The Murderdance. Immediately after this model Charges, it can make a single additional attack with one of its melee weapons.
                     * Unbound Arrogance. Whenever this model makes a melee attack, you and your opponent each secretly choose a number between 1 and 3, then simultaneously reveal your choices. If the numbers differ, this model has +DICE Equal to the number you chose Hit with that attack. If they are the same, this model has -1 DICE to Hit instead.
Patron: Tzeentch
Or a powerful Tzeentch Daemon.
                     * Aetherstride. This model ignores Difficult and Dangerous Terrain, and does not suffer Injury rolls due to falling, nor does it suffer an Injury roll due to failing a Diving Charge.
                     * Arrogance Of Aeons (Psyker Only). This model has +1 DICE to Deny the Witch, and can do so at a range of up to 18”.
                     * Fractal Mind (Psyker Only). This model can choose to use an additional psychic power before beginning to count how many powers it has used during its Activation. That power has PERILOUS ±1.
                     * Incorporeal Form. Injury rolls made against this model have an additional -1 DICE unless they have the FIRE, GAS, or PSYCHIC Keyword.
                     * Lorekeeper Of Tzeentch (Psyker Only). The range of all of this model’s psychic powers is increased by 6”.
                     * Tyrant of the Warp (Psyker Only). This model does not suffer Perils of the Warp unless the power it is using has PERILOUS, but reduce the PERILOUS value by 1. (PERILOUS ±0 still allows it to suffer Perils of the Warp on a 2 or 12.)
Patron: Heretek Lord
Vashtor the Arkifane, the master of a Hell-Forge, or a similar master of dark technology.
                     * Architect Of Destruction. This model’s weapons with BLAST increase their BLAST radius by 2”.
                     * Bastion. Enemies that Charged during this Turn have -1 INJURY DICE this model, and cannot force this model to move.
                     * Daemonsmith. Injury rolls made against friendly VEHICLE models within 3” of this model have -1 DICE.
                     * Implacable Taskmaster. The first time this model moves during each of its Activations, one friendly model within 6” of it can also move up to half of its own movement speed. This movement can bring that model into close combat, but also provides free attacks as if it Retreated if it leaves close combat.
                     * Siege Lord. If the target of any of this model’s ranged attack would have an armour modifier of -2 or higher, you may set one die of the injury roll to a 6 before the roll is made. The rest of the dice for the injury roll are rolled as normal.
                     * Unyielding Mettle. Up to the first -2 of this model’s armour has IMPERVIOUS.
Shared Outlaw Patrons
Patron: Pirate Lord
                     * The Boot. Downed Enemy models in Melee Combat with this model cannot automatically Stand at the start of their Activation.
                     * Pillager. This model gains the Re-roll Exploration Skill twice.
                     * Port Contacts. While this model is in your warband, you can increase your LIMIT for any weapon, armour, or equipment by 1. Each piece of battlekit can only be increased once in this way.
                     * Scar Collector. This model can suffer up to 2 additional Battle Scars before being killed.
                     * Walk the Plank. Enemy models within 3” of this model automatically fail all Success Rolls made to avoid falling when Downed, and suffer +1 DICE INJURY to the Injury roll caused by such a fall.
                     * Whale Hunter. When this model makes a Melee Attack Success Roll against a LARGE enemy, they roll 3d6 to hit instead of 2d6, totalling the sum, to Hit.
