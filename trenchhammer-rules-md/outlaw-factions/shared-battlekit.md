Shared Armour
Shared Armour
                     * Standard Armour. -1 INJURY MODIFIER
                     * Heavy Armour. -2 INJURY MODIFIER, HEAVY. Due to its bulk, even a STRONG wearer rolls only a D3 for extra charge distance instead of a D6.
                     * Power Armour. -2 INJURY MODIFIER
                     * Terminator Armour. -3 INJURY MODIFIER. Model gains the DEEP STRIKE, STRONG, and VEHICLE Keywords, as well as LARGE if not already LARGE. Due to its bulk the wearer rolls only a D3 for extra charge distance instead of a D6. Terminator Armour cannot be combined with a Shield, nor used with a Jump Pack.
                     * Shield. -1 INJURY MODIFIER, HELD
                     * Heavy Shield. HELD, HEAVY, COVER. Counts as a Shield for the purposes of the Shield Combo property.

Cyberteknika Equipment
Cyberteknika cannot be removed or sold once given to a model. If the model suffers an injury of the type their Cyberteknika prevents, roll a D6. On a 1-3, the Cyberteknika is destroyed, but the injury (and Scar) is not sustained. An ELITE model can have up to 3 Cyberteknika. Other models can have up to 2 Cyberteknika.
                     * Cranial Cyberteknika. Critical Hits do not grant the normal +1 DICE against this model. The CRITICAL Keyword still functions normally. In addition, the model ignores the penalties of the following Traumas (but not the accompanying Scar) and cannot gain them: Dark Memory, Head Wound, Paranoia.
                     * Ocular Cyberteknika. The model ignores the penalty for shooting at Long Range. In addition, the model ignores the penalties of the following Traumas (but not the accompanying Scar) and cannot gain them: Insomniac, Lost an Eye.
                     * Sindexterous Cyberteknika. The model ignores the penalty for attack with its off-hand. In addition, the model ignores the penalties of the following Traumas (but not the accompanying Scar) and cannot gain them: Hand Wound, Lost Arm.
                     * Motive Cyberteknika. The model has +1 DICE to Climb, Jump, and Diving Charge Success Rolls, as well as Success Rolls to avoid falling off of raised terrain. In addition, the model ignores the penalties of the following Traumas (but not the accompanying Scar) and cannot gain them: Leg Wounded, Muscle Damage.
                     * Torsonic Cyberteknika. Injuries against the model are rolled with -1 DICE. In addition, the model ignores the penalties of the following Traumas (but not the accompanying Scar) and cannot gain them: Chest Wound.
                     * Vascular Cyberteknika. The model has +1 DICE to all Dash Success Rolls. In addition, the model ignores the penalties of the following Traumas (but not the accompanying Scar) and cannot gain them: Severe Nerve Damage, Shell-Shocked.
