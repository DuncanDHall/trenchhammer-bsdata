# Changelog

Beta 1.3.3 (in progress)
Updated wording to Trench Crusade version 1.0.2.
* BURROW is now a Keyword instead of a movement type.
* Added FLYING Keyword to flying models.
* Replaced MULTISTRIKE with CLEAVE, now that it exists (they do the same thing).
* Updated 3d6 Injury weapons to DEADLY Keyword.
* Updated mine-setters to use MINED Keyword and gave them NEGATE MINED.
* Updated regenerating models to REGENERATE Keyword.
* Updated ammunition equipment to AMMUNITION Keyword.
* Updated Pistoleer style abilities to new wording from the skill.
* Updated Skill & Expertise Skill, but cannot choose Dash, and can choose battlekit actions.
* Resist and Bite Glorious Deed updated.
* Glorious Deeds that no model is involved in give XP to the Warband Leader (or any Elite with LEADER, technically).
* Patron skills that increase the limit for models or battlekit no longer continue applying if the model with the skill is removed from your Warband.
General
* Added SWEEPING Keyword to simplify weapons that hit every enemy in melee.
* Various wording and typo fixes and clarifications.
Battlekit
* Stakethrower cost reduced cost to 10 from 15 in all factions that have it.
* Replaced old GRENADE Keyword with IGNORE COVER and IGNORE LONG RANGE.
Psychic Powers
* Models with PSYKER Keyword cost reduced all around.
* Most PSYKER models can take powers from only one Discipline and no longer choose an additional Shared one.
* Many psychic powers updated in a variety of ways for better balance.
Shared Patrons
* Fixed missing Lord Inquisitor skill, moved some skill effects around.
Adepta Sororitas
* Added Mortisanctus upgrade for battle sisters.
* Heavy Power Weapon no longer has LIMIT, but is Mortisanctus or Paragon Warsuit only.
* Hand Flamers fixed so they can be used by Battle Sisters.
* Repentia can take Purity Seals. (Why not?)
Adeptus Astartes
* Basic Astartes model renamed to Space Marine to avoid confusion with Keyword.
Aeldari
* Wraithseer fixed to have 60mm base, -3 Armour, and +10 cost.
Astra Militarum
* Death Corp Sappers cost +15 instead of +20.
Genestealer Cults
* Broodlord has N/A Ranged Skill and costs 10 less, as its only ranged attacks uses its PSYKER Keyword.
Heretic Astartes
* Basic Heretic Astartes model renamed to Chaos Space Marine to avoid confusion with Keyword.
* Helbrute Hammer adds INJURY MODIFIER instead of INJURY DICE.
* Power Scourge uses SWEEPING Keyword and has AP 1.
Inquisition
* Has the full extra 11 Glory for one-off games instead of just 7.
Necrons
* Lokhust Lord and Skorpekh Lord have +3/+3 skill each, but cost 15 more.
T’au Empire
* Ethereal’s Wisdom of the Guides invocation effect replaced.
Tyranids
* Feed the Swarm ability for Assimilation Swarm simplified and works like a Medicae Kit.
* Zoanthrope has N/A Ranged Skill and costs 5 less, as its only ranged attacks uses its PSYKER Keyword.

Aeldari

Genestealer Cults

Heretic Astartes

T’au Empire

Beta 1.3.1
General
* Reduced the cost of some melee-only models.
* Bladed Stance and similar abilities (except the Succubus, who has to pay to use it) can only be used by a model that has not acted yet, and costs its attack, but is a full Fight Action.
Psychic Powers
* Psychic Powers have been reformatted, PSYKER now has an X value assigned that determines the +DICE used for using powers, replacing abilities that allow additional powers before suffering penalties, and is used for attack powers instead of ranged/melee skill.
* Added PERILOUS Keyword that modifies the numbers needed to suffer Perils of the Warp.
* Use of multiple powers causes PERILOUS in addition to -DICE.
* Models with PSYKER 2 or 3 have increased cost.
* Deny the Witch slightly simplified.
Battlekit
* Hand flamers lose FLAMETHROWER in melee.
* Removed minimums from ARMOUR PIERCING.
* Lightning Claws cost 12 instead of 15.
* Added MAIN HAND ONLY Keyword. Some weapons were tweaked to accommodate.
* Heavy Shield grants cover instead of -1D Injury, as with Heavy Ballistic Shield in core game. Praesidium Shield loses -1D to be Injured, but adds IMPERVIOUS to -1 of armour.
* Thunder Hammer has +2 INJURY MODIFIER instead of +1 and AP 1.
* Renamed Power Fist to Heavy Power Fist, to be less confusing. Normal Power Fist is just a Power Weapon like any other.
Mercenaries
* Barhesi Raider is now an Outlaw mercenary, but Drukhari can still take it.
* Added Barghesi Thrall Outlaw, also available to Drukhari.
* Removed ELITE from Dialogus, to prevent it from becoming a must-take for Sororitas who benefit more from elites now.
Heretic Astartes
* Chaos Lord not STRONG by default, costs 10 less.
* Mark of Nurgle is ignored by Bloodbaths. Mark of Slaanesh gives +2” movement instead of +1”.
* God-specific Marks of Chaos cost 15 credits instead of 2 Glory.
* Death Guard’s Inevitable rule moved to Mark of Nurgle. Contagion ability is now extra, so they cost 5 more.
* Great Plague Blade gets power weapon armour piercing, costs +10.
* Limit 1 Thunder Hammer.
* Thousand Sons can recruit 2 Chaos Sorcerers instead of 3. (This was intended originally, but Exalted Sorcerer became its one model instead of an upgrade.)
* Bestial Prophet does not apply to self.
Sororitas and Ministorum
* Tweaks to Miracles rule and Sororitas elite abilities.
* Null Rod and Null Skull updated to add FEAR and Deny the Witch instead of an aura of no psychic powers.
* Repentia pay less for new Power Armour when promoted, but Battle Sisters’ armour becomes “reserved” while they are demoted.
Astartes
* Iron Halo costs 4 Glory instead of 2.
* Dreadnought equipment options clarified and simplified.
* Limit 1 Thunder Hammer.
* Great Frost Axe gets +2 INJURY DICE instead of +1, but loses AP1.
Custodes
* Custodes get STRONG, cost 5 more, but their main weapons get HEAVY and cost 5 less. Mostly this lets them use normal two-handed weapons in one hand.
* Custodes’ combined weapons modified to be closer to Power Weapons, prices re-evaluated.
* Custodes get Frag and Krak Grenades.
* Contemptor Dreadnoughts equipment options clarified and simplified.
Gene Stealer Cults
* Abominant hits all enemies in melee.
Necrons
* Destroyer Cult unique weapons tweaked and re-costed.
* Flayed Ones cost 70 instead of 75 or 80 for Court of the Flayer King and Destroyer Cult.
* Plasmacytes are now once per battle, but take no action or roll, and Accelerators give +2 DICE to the action. Accelerators cost 10 instead of 15.
* Resurrection Orb doesn’t require a roll.
Tyranids
* Shadow in the Warp causes increased DICE penalty to using multiple powers, since PERILOUS does not matter.
* Neuroloids’ location can be used as the Neurotyrant’s for psychic powers, but cost 45.
Pirate Crew
* Restricted Ork Campaign Shop items to ORK pirates.
* More specialization options.
